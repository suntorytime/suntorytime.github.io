by Santa Maria, Steven * July 10, 2015

Recently, I had the opportunity to get a better understanding of DBC's culture though a fireside chat with Shereef. In many ways it's unique in that it stresses the emotional attitude one should have entering DBC.

Here is how I approach other aspects of the culture at DBC:

Based on the video, what's your take on the DBC experience?

I feel that the DBC experience is up my alley. For many years, I thought coding was a skill that I simply could not approach because I didn't have the foresight to have taken it during my high school or undergraduate careers. According to the fireside chat, DBC fosters a democratic, collaborative learning environment.
